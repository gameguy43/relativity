RELATIVITY

Relativity makes it fun to develop an intuition for relative sizes.

To play:
  1. Break into 2 groups and line up. The person in the front of each line is the speaker for that group
  2. You have 5 seconds to guess the number of smaller things (LEFT) that fit into the bigger thing (RIGHT). Everyone shout out guesses!

  4. The team that guesses closest gets a point
  5. Speakers to the back of the line—begin again!
